    • FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



    Q1. What is the registration fee?
    Ans. The registration fee is Rs 7000.

    Q2. Is the registration fee refundable, post-registration?
    Ans. No, the registration fee is non-refundable.

    Q3. Will the accommodation be provided by the organizers?
    Ans. Yes, the accommodation is provided by the organizers. The registration fee is inclusive of the same.

    Q4. Are the coaches allowed to accompany the teams during the moot?
    Ans. No, the coaches cannot accompany the teams.

    Q5. Will the travel expense be covered in the fee?
    Ans. No, the participants would have to bear the travel expenses.

    Q6. What would be the dates of the accommodation?
    Ans. The dates of the accommodation provided will be from 16th - 19th February 2023. Check out will be on the morning of 19th February.

    Q7. What happens if one of the teammates cannot participate in the competition?
    Ans. Generally, no alternates are allowed in the competition. However, in case of emergency, contact the organizers as soon as possible.

    Q8. When will the exchange of memorials happen?
    Ans. For preliminary and reverse preliminary rounds, the memorial exchange shall happen a day in advance. Teams reaching the Octa-Finals, Quarter-Finals and Semi-Final rounds will be provided with the memorial of the opposing team a few hours before the scheduled round. Lastly, for final rounds, the exchange of memorial shall happen a day in advance following the declaration of results of the Semi-Final Rounds.

    Q9. Are teams allowed to change their speaker or researcher roles?
    Ans. No, change of Speaker or Researcher role is not allowed. Such a change can only be allowed in exceptional circumstances, at the discretion of the Organizing Committee.

    Q10. What to do in case the amount is debited but the status of payment is still pending?
    Ans. For any payment related issue, please contact the Organizing Committee.
