    THE NLU ANTITRUST LAW MOOT COURT COMPETITION

    The NLU Antitrust Law Moot Court Competition (“Moot”), an initiative conceived to generate interest in competition law amongst law students across the nation, is the flagship moot court competition of National Law University Jodhpur. Participation in the Moot entails engaging in research on contemporary issues pertaining to competition law and pleading a hypothetical dispute before the appropriate forum. The NLU Antitrust Law Moot Court Competition is the first moot court competition in India in the area of competition law. The University has successfully organized twelve previous editions of the Moot.

    The Thirteenth edition of the Moot was organized from 11th -13th March, 2022 virtually over the Cisco Webex Meetings platform. It was hosted by the University under the patronage of the Competition Commission of India and in association with Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas and our knowledge partners, SCC Online and Eastern Book Company, and witnessed 37 participating teams from some of the country’s most prestigious law schools.

    The Final Rounds of the Thirteenth Edition were judged by a panel of distinguished judges, comprising Dr. Geeta Gouri (Former member, Competition Commission of India), Mr. Yaman Verma (Partner, Shardul Amarchand Mangaldas & Co.), Ms. Payal Malik (Adviser and Head of Economics Division, Competition Commission of India), Prof. Harpreet Kaur (Professor of Law and Registrar National Law University, Delhi) and Mr. Ram Kumar Poornachandran (Partner, AZB & Partners). As competition law is a unique and niche area of law, the Moot is considered to be one of the most prestigious and coveted moot court competitions in India.
